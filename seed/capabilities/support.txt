Meridian Systems — Support Capabilities

SUPPORT TIERS

Starter
- Channels: Email only
- Response SLA: 48 business hours
- Coverage: Business hours (Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm Gulf Standard Time)
- Self-service: Full access to knowledge base and community forum
- No dedicated customer success manager

Professional
- Channels: Email and live chat
- Response SLA: 8 business hours
- Coverage: Business hours (Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm Gulf Standard Time)
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM): Yes. CSM is the named point of contact for all non-emergency issues, onboarding questions, and feature guidance.
- Quarterly Business Reviews: Yes. CSM schedules and runs a structured QBR every 90 days covering platform utilisation, open issues, and upcoming feature roadmap.
- Escalation path: CSM escalates to engineering within 4 business hours for P1 issues

Enterprise
- Channels: Email, live chat, and phone (24/7)
- Response SLA: 2 hours (24/7, including weekends and public holidays)
- Coverage: 24/7/365
- Named Support Engineer: Yes. A named Meridian engineer is assigned to the account and has context on the customer's configuration and integration setup.
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager: Yes
- Quarterly Business Reviews: Yes
- Escalation path: Named support engineer escalates directly to product engineering for P1 issues; target resolution within 8 hours for P1
- Executive escalation: Available for Enterprise customers — CSM can escalate to Meridian VP of Customer Success within 1 business hour if unresolved after initial SLA

PRIORITY LEVELS

P1 — Critical: Platform unavailable or data inaccessible. All tiers: immediate escalation. Enterprise: 2-hour response.
P2 — High: Core functionality degraded. Enterprise: 4-hour response. Professional: next business day.
P3 — Medium: Non-critical functionality affected, workaround available. All tiers: within SLA.
P4 — Low: Feature requests, questions, general guidance. All tiers: within SLA.

HYPERCARE SUPPORT (post go-live)

During the hypercare period (30 days standard, 45 days extended), all customers receive elevated support regardless of tier:
- 4-hour response SLA for all issues
- Weekly check-in calls with implementation manager
- Priority access to configuration change requests

STATUS PAGE

Real-time platform status available at status.meridiansystems.io. Includes current status of all platform components, historical uptime data, and incident history. Customers can subscribe to email or webhook notifications for status changes.

Uptime SLA: 99.9% (Professional), 99.95% (Enterprise), measured monthly.
